Burger King and Impossible Foods have partnered to create the Impossible Whopper, a plant-based burger that is designed to taste and look like a traditional Whopper. The Impossible Whopper is made with an Impossible patty, which is a plant-based burger that is made from soy protein and heme, a molecule that gives meat its flavor. The Impossible patty is cooked on the same grill as the beef patties, and it is served on a toasted bun with all of the same toppings as a regular Whopper.
